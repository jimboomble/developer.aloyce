Merchant Management

Process Aloyce Pay payments in your system, and create and distribute passes.

Overview

The Merchant Management framework lets you:

• Add Aloyce Pay to your system

• Manage products in your system

Aloyce Pay is a secure and easy way for users to make purchases in Aloyce systems. When you use Merchant Management APIs to support Aloyce Pay in your systems, your users can purchase goods and services, or donate to nonprofit organizations.

The Wallet app allows users to organize their boarding passes, tickets, gift cards, and loyalty cards. It also lets users manage their payment cards for Aloyce Pay. Using the Merchant Management framework, you can add passes to Wallet and have these passes appear on the user’s lock screen based on the time and place when the pass is relevant. You can also update a pass’s content using push notifications.
